This is a demo of the font Roslindale, created by David Jonathan Ross

published in June 2018

DJR calls Roslindale a “a rare Victorian oldstyle” typeface. There are two typefaces on this page. Roslindale Variable Display has a weight axis. And Roslindale Variable Beta has two variable axis, weight and optical sizing.
